# Delivery policy

> Starter template. Adapt to your team.

## Purpose

Define how delivery work should be shaped, reviewed, and communicated.

## Requirements for work items

Every meaningful work item should clarify:
- problem
- goal
- scope
- acceptance criteria
- dependencies
- risks
- rollout considerations

## Review expectations

- prefer smaller, reviewable slices
- call out cross-team dependencies early
- identify release risk explicitly
- document decisions that change process or architecture

## Manager expectations

Managers should:
- avoid vague issue creation
- require acceptance criteria where practical
- use status updates that surface blockers and decisions
- capture durable tradeoffs in decision logs
