# Meeting to decision-log playbook

## Use when
A meeting produced:
- a meaningful decision
- a durable tradeoff
- an approval
- a rejected option worth recording

## Process
1. summarize the meeting
2. extract actions
3. identify whether a real decision occurred
4. create a decision log if yes
5. update glossary / FAQ if the discussion introduced durable terminology or recurring questions

## Do not create a decision log for
- simple status updates
- one-off administrative notes
- decisions with no future relevance
