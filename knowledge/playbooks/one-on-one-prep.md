# One-on-one preparation playbook

## Goal
Prepare focused one-on-ones that surface:
- progress
- blockers
- decisions
- growth topics
- follow-up actions

## Suggested structure
- wins since last meeting
- blockers or risk
- decisions needed
- coaching / growth
- follow-ups

## Copilot usage
Use Copilot to:
- summarize previous notes
- identify open follow-ups
- draft a concise agenda
- propose questions based on recent work
