# Operating model

> Template starter. Adapt to your organization.

## Weekly rhythm

### Monday
- planning and prioritization
- issue refinement

### Mid-week
- delivery review
- unblock work
- capture decisions

### Friday
- status summary
- risk review
- update docs and playbooks if the team learned something durable

## How this repo is used during the week

- rough requests become issues
- meeting notes become action lists or decision logs
- new guidance becomes sidecar summaries
- repeated questions become FAQ entries
- new terminology becomes glossary entries

## Review cadence

### Weekly
- FAQ review
- glossary additions
- stale prompt check

### Monthly
- Space curation review
- adoption / enablement review
- policy and workflow check
