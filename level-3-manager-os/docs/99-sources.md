## Evidence quality notes

- The GitHub, Microsoft, VS Code, and DORA sources are product or research primary sources and should be treated as the strongest guidance for workflow design.
- The McKinsey sources are broad management research, not GitHub-specific guidance.
- The Clockwise benchmark is vendor research. It is useful directionally for engineering-manager meeting load, but it should not be treated as a universal benchmark for every org.
